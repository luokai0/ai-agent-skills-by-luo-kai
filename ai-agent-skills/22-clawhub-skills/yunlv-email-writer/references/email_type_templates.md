- Price: [价格] USD/pc [价格条款]
- MOQ: [最小起订量]
- Lead time: [交期]

Sample available: Yes/No
Sample cost: [费用]

## 四、付款催款邮件

### 第一阶段：友好提醒
```
Subject: Gentle Reminder - Invoice #[编号] Due

Dear [客户姓名],

I hope this message finds you well.

This is a friendly reminder that Invoice #[编号] for [金额] USD is due on [到期日].

Payment details:
Bank: [银行]
Account Name: [账户名]
Account No.: [账号]

Please confirm when payment has been arranged.

Thank you for your continued partnership!

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```

### 第二阶段：正式催款
```
Subject: Urgent: Invoice #[编号] Overdue - Action Required

Dear [客户姓名],

I hope you are doing well.

I'm following up regarding Invoice #[编号] ([金额] USD) which was due on [到期日].

We haven't received the payment yet. Could you please:
1. Confirm the payment status
2. Provide the bank transfer record

If there are any issues or disputes, please let us know immediately so we can resolve them.

Your prompt attention to this matter would be highly appreciated.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```
