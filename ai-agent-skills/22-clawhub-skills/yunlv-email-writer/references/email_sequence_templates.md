# 邮件序列模板

## 一、询盘回复序列（5步）

| 步骤 | 时间 | 邮件类型 | 内容重点 |
|-----|------|---------|---------|
| 1 | Day 0 | 初始回复 | 感谢+报价+样品说明 |
| 2 | Day 3 | 价值强调 | 产品优势+客户案例 |
| 3 | Day 7 | 跟进 | 询问进展+解决方案 |
| 4 | Day 14 | 优惠 | 限时优惠/批量折扣 |
| 5 | Day 21 | 最后跟进 | 再次价值主张 |

### 第一封：初始回复
```
Subject: Re: [客户询盘主题] - Quotation Attached

Hi [Name],

Thank you for your inquiry about [产品]!

I've attached our competitive quotation with the following highlights:
✓ [优势1]
✓ [优势2]
✓ [优势3]

Sample available: Yes, [费用], [交期]

Please let me know if you need any clarification.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```

### 第二封：价值强调（Day 3）
```
Subject: RE: [产品] - Why Our Customers Choose Us

Hi [Name],

Just following up on my previous email.

I'd like to share why companies like [同行案例] choose us:
- [客户收益1]
- [客户收益2]

We're also proud to serve [知名客户] for [年数] years.

Looking forward to your feedback!

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```

### 第三封：解决方案（Day 7）
```
Subject: Re: [产品] - Addressing Your Concerns?

Hi [Name],

I know you might have concerns about [潜在顾虑].

Here's how we've helped similar companies:
[解决方案/案例]

Would a sample help you evaluate? 

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```

### 第四封：优惠（Day 14）
```
Subject: Special Offer - [产品] Trial Order

Hi [Name],

I'd like to offer you an exclusive trial:
- [特殊优惠内容]
- Valid until [日期]

This is our way to build a long-term partnership.

Interested?

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```

### 第五封：最后跟进（Day 21）
```
Subject: Final Follow-up - [产品]

Hi [Name],

I don't want to overwhelm your inbox!

If [产品] isn't a priority right now, I'll understand. But if timing changes, I'm here to help.

Feel free to save my contact for future reference.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```
